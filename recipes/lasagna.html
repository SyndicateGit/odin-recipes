Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna. Garfield's desired dish.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets: Store Bought Amount: 5-6
  • Onion and garlic
  • Sugar: White Sugar Amount: 2 Tablespoons
  • Cheese: Ricotta/Mozzarella/Parmesan Amount: Heart's Desire
  • Egg: Any kind Amount: 1
  • Stuffing: Any ground meat or sausage / Soft vegetables. Amount: 100g

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta/cheese mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
